Spacebar:
	-Toggles Day and Night.
		(Day is a strange blue because
		it is raining.)

L Key:
	-Triggers a lightning flash.

R Key:
	-Toggles rain.
	-Changes day/night colors to fit rain better.
	-Turns lightning flashes off.
		(This includes the L key.)
	-Disables the umbrella.
		(Does not stop it from moving.)
Right:
	-Makes the umbrella go right or stop moving if it was traveling left.
Left:
	-Makes the umbrella go left or stop moving if it was traveling right.
Up:
	-Makes the umbrella go up or stop moving if it was traveling down.
Down:
	-Makes the umbrella go down or stop moving if it was traveling up.
